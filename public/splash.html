Run your tattoo shop with ease.

Are you a tattoo artist, or do you manage a tattoo shop? Artisan Assistant is the simplest way to manage your client information.

All of the information in your client's file is searchable. From a client's name to a random phone number you scribbled on a drawing, Artisan Assistant will look it up in your client database.

You will be able to check on your client's upcoming appointment, and Artisan Assistant will even send an automated text reminder to your client.

Do you travel for conventions? Artisan Assistant can search client files by city so you can contact them when you are in their town again.
